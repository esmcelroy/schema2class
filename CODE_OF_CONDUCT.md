# Code of Conduct

schema2class follows a simple standard: be respectful, constructive, and
focused on improving the project.

## Expected Behavior

- Treat other contributors with patience and professional courtesy.
- Give technical feedback that is specific, actionable, and about the work.
- Assume good intent, but make room for correction when impact differs from
  intent.
- Respect maintainers' decisions about scope, priority, and release timing.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, intimidation, or sustained disruption.
- Insults, personal attacks, or discriminatory language.
- Publishing private information without consent.
- Repeatedly pushing an argument after a maintainer has made a final decision.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit or remove comments, close issues or pull requests, block
participants, or take other reasonable action to keep the project usable and
welcoming.

Report conduct concerns privately to `esmcelroy@hey.com`. Include links,
screenshots, or other context when possible.
